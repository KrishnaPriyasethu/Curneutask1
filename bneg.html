B negative blood type

B negative blood is one of the rarest blood types as just 2% of the donor population has it.

Who can receive B negative blood?

B negative red blood cells can be used to treat patients with:

  • B negative blood
  • B positive blood
  • AB positive blood
  • AB negative blood

    What blood can B negative people receive?

    People with B negative blood can only receive red cell donations from B negative donors and O negative donors.
