A negative blood type

Around 8% of donors have A negative blood..It is a rare blood group

Who can receive A negative blood?

A negative red blood cells can be used to treat patients with:

  • A negative blood
  • A positive blood
  • AB positive blood
  • AB negative blood

    What blood can A negative people receive?

    People with A negative blood can only receive red cell donations from A negative donors and O negative donors.
