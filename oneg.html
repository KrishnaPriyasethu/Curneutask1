O negative blood type

Around 8% of the donor population has O negative blood.It is a rare blood group

Who can receive O negative blood?

O negative blood is often called the ‘universal blood type’ because people of any blood type can receive it. This makes it vitally important in an emergency or when a patient’s blood type is unknown.

What blood can O positive people receive?

People with O negative blood can only receive red cell donations from O negative donors.
