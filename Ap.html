A positive blood type

Around 30% of donors have A positive blood, making it the second most common blood type after O positive (36%).

Who can receive A positive blood?

A positive red blood cells can be given to people with A positive blood and AB positive blood.

What blood can A positive people receive?

People with A positive blood can receive donations from A positive donors, A negative donors, O negative donors and O positive donors
