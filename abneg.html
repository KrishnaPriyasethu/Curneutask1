AB negative blood type

AB negative blood is one of the rarest blood types as just 1% of the donor population has it.

Who can receive AB negative blood?

AB negative red blood cells can be used to treat patients with:

  • AB positive blood
  • AB negative blood

    What blood can AB negative people receive?

    People with AB negative blood can only receive red cell donations from B negative donors,A negative donors,AB negative donors and O negative donors.
